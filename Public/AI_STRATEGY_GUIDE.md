# The Forge: AI Strategic Companion Guide

Welcome to the cutting edge of Magic: The Gathering intelligence. **The Forge** isn't just a database; it's a strategic ecosystem powered by advanced AI and Actionable Intelligence (AI).

This guide details the core engines that give you the edge in brewing and gameplay.

---

## 1. The AI Deck Doctor
*Precision Analysis. Professional Results.*

Most power level calculators use static heuristics. The **Deck Doctor** uses a multi-layered analysis engine to surgically evaluate your deck.

### The 1-5 Bracket System
We've replaced vague 1-10 scores with a specialized 5-tier bracket system:
- **Bracket 1: Casual/Precon** - Focused on flavor and fundamental synergy.
- **Bracket 2: Optimized** - Efficient interaction and consistent win-cons.
- **Bracket 3: High Power** - Fast-paced strategy with specialized threats.
- **Bracket 4: Fringe Competitive** - Near-optimal efficiency and speed.
- **Bracket 5: Maximum Power (cEDH)** - Zero-compromise strategic execution.

### Surgical Swaps
The Deck Doctor doesn't just critique; it prescribes. Our AI scans your available collection to suggest specific card-for-card swaps to:
- **Smooth your mana curve.**
- **Increase interaction density.**
- **Strengthen mechanical themes.**

## 3. Pod Synergy & Group Intelligence
*Rule Your Pod.*

Magic is a social game. The Forge extends its AI capabilities to your entire playgroup:
- **Shared Collection Analysis**: The AI can suggest cards from your friends' binders for trades that benefit both players.
- **Pod Power Balancing**: Ensure everyone in your pod is playing at the same bracket for the best game experience.
- **Group Statistics**: Track the evolving meta of your specific playgroup.
